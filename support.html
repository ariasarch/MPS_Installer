Support & Troubleshooting

Common fixes and where to get help.

General Tips

  • Keep your raw data outside the MPS working directory.
  • Read the Terminal window—errors include guidance and log paths.
  • For bug reports, include OS version, what you clicked, and the last 50 lines of the log.

macOS

  • Gatekeeper prompt: Right-click the app → Open (one-time).
  • Conda missing: Accept the Miniforge prompt; relaunch after install.
  • App won’t start: Delete ~/Library/Application Support/MPS/env and reopen (env will be recreated).
  • Logs: ~/Library/Application Support/MPS/logs/launcher_YYYY-MM-DD_*.log

Windows

  • SmartScreen: More info → Run anyway.
  • Permission errors: Run installer as Administrator.
  • Slow install: Network/package solving—let it finish.
